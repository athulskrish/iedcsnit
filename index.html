A blend of Technology and Entrepreneurship

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC) at Sree Narayana Institute of Technology (SNIT) is a dynamic hub fostering innovation, creativity, and entrepreneurial spirit among students and the broader community. Established with the vision of nurturing aspiring entrepreneurs, the IEDC at SNIT provides a supportive ecosystem, offering guidance, mentorship, and resources to transform innovative ideas into impactful ventures. Our center aims to inspire, educate, and empower the next generation of entrepreneurs through a variety of workshops, mentorship programs, networking events, and access to valuable resources. We are committed to cultivating an environment that encourages experimentation, learning, and the cultivation of entrepreneurial skills, positioning our students to thrive in the rapidly evolving business landscape.

Workshops and Entrepreneurship Talks

"Join us for an exhilarating series of workshops and engaging entrepreneurship talks hosted by IEDC. Our workshops are designed to inspire, educate, and empower individuals seeking to delve into the world of entrepreneurship. Whether you're an aspiring business owner, a seasoned entrepreneur, or someone curious about innovative ideas and strategies, these sessions offer invaluable insights, practical guidance, and networking opportunities. Through dynamic discussions led by industry experts and successful entrepreneurs, participants will gain essential skills, discover emerging trends, and learn from real-world experiences. Explore the endless possibilities and ignite your entrepreneurial spirit with our thought-provoking workshops and talks at IEDC."
